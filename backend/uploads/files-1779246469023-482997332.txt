Mathematics Past Paper
Year: 2024
Section A: Algebra
Q1. Solve the quadratic equation x^2 - 5x + 6 = 0. Find the values of x.
Q2. Simplify the algebraic expression (2x + 3)(x - 4) - (x^2 - 2).
Q3. Find the roots of the equation 3x^2 + 2x - 1 = 0 using the quadratic formula.

Section B: Trigonometry
Q4. Prove the trigonometric identity: sin^2(theta) + cos^2(theta) = 1.
Q5. Find the value of sin(60) * cos(30) + cos(60) * sin(30).
Q6. In a right-angled triangle, if tan(theta) = 3/4, find sin(theta) and cos(theta).

Section C: Calculus
Q7. Find the derivative of the function f(x) = 3x^3 - 5x^2 + 2x - 7 with respect to x.
Q8. Evaluate the definite integral of (2x + 3) from x = 1 to x = 3.
Q9. Find the limit as x approaches 2 of (x^2 - 4) / (x - 2).
